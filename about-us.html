Becky

Becky , unlike lucy, grew up in Sweden where sandwiches were eaten for breakfast. therefore the idea of eating such a meal for lunch or dinner was simply unheard of and quite frankly rather ridiculous. However, since moving toLondon where eating almost nothing besides avocado and chia seeds is the norm, beckys craving for delicious doughy goodness in the form of a burger sky rocketed. Under the guidance of fellow burger fiend (aka Lucy) Becky has developed a new found appreciation for the burg, one that has allowed her acceptance into the burg city bitch crew.
